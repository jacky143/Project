Hello, and welcome to Robbie's Farm!

Robbie's farm was founded in 1960 and has been in the family ever since. Many people may see us as just a farm, however we are more than just a farm. This farm repersents our family history and is a staple in the community. Each generation of my family has contributed to the history of the farm and the community. Our farm was one of the first in the community to sell our produce in town at an afforable price. Our farm was also the first to give left over produce to churces and other enities in the community. For generations we have prided our selves with producing the best tasting produce at an affordable price. Although we do not allow vistors at this time we still want to share our familys farm history with the community.
